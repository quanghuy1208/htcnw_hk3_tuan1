A smile is a facial expression formed primarily by flexing the muscles at the sides of the mouth. It is believed it takes about 80 muscles to smile. Some include a contraction of the muscles at the corner of the eyes, an action knows as a "Duchenne smile". Smiles performes without the eye contraction may be perceived as insincere.
